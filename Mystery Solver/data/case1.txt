WHO SOLD JOSEPH?

In the land of Canaan, a favoured son named Joseph wore a coat of many colors,a gift from his father
that bred jealousy among his eleven brothers.

They plotted against him, first to kill, then one suggested a different fate - to sell him 
to Ishmaelite traders passing by Dothan's gate.


Your guest is to discover which of Joseph's brothers made this suggestion, and uncover the 
truth behind this ancient transgression.
